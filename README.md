## **Overview**

The **AI Risk Escalation System for Fraud Signals** is a web-based tool that helps fraud analysts automatically assess the risk of financial transactions, recommend appropriate actions, and escalate only critical cases for human review. The system combines AI-powered risk scoring with human-in-the-loop approval for high-impact decisions, ensuring compliance, accountability, and operational efficiency.

---

## **Features**

### **Transaction Input & Scenarios**
- Enter transaction details manually or choose from predefined risk scenarios (**low**, **medium**, **high**).

### **AI Risk Assessment**
- Generates a **risk score** and **risk level**.
- Provides **recommended actions** with **reasoning** and a draft **customer message**.

### **Human-in-the-Loop Escalation**
- Critical actions, such as **permanent account freezes**, require **analyst approval** before execution.

### **Audit Logs & Analytics**
- Logs all AI decisions, human approvals, and transaction data.
- View **historical transactions** and visualize **risk trends** with interactive charts.
- Export logs to **CSV** for reporting or compliance purposes.
